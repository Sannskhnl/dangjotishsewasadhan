Biography

Megharaj Acharya

Everyone has their own journey, and Megharaj Acharya, also known as Toofan Guru, has a story filled with valuable advice that teaches us a lot.


Megharaj Acharya, born in the enchanting village of Jaljala, Dhairing, Parbat, Nepal, entered the world to parents Jamuna Upadhyaya and Chakrapani Upadhyaya. Challenges arose at the tender age of 9 when his father passed away. Amidst the trials, Megharaj found strength in his family, which includes two elder brothers, Surya Acharya and Kedar Acharya, two younger brothers, Bhojraj Acharya and Mahodata Acharya, along with an eldest sister, Mithu Subedi, and a younger sister, Laxmi Pokhrel. Faced challenges in his early studies but worked hard doing village tasks like fieldwork. After completing class 6, his family sent him to the unfamiliar place of Dang. With only Rs: 200, he went to Dang with his friend Dinesh Acharya. To make a living, he stayed in a home where he cooked, gave tuition to small children, and thus, his journey in Dang started.
